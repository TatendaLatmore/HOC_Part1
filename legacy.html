Legacy

Alan Turing is widely recognized for his contributions to computing and artificial intelligence, Alan Turing's legacy extends far beyond these points, influencing the realms of computer science, artificial intelligence, cryptography, and beyond. His ideas and contributions continue to shape the way we think about computation and intelligence in the modern world.
